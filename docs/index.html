All the game energy,
without the clutter.

Designed to feel like a modern sports-bar ticker on your own desktop.

01

Live Score Movement

Follow every active game with smooth, always-on motion. Real-time scores, outs, and base runners update automatically.

02

Deep Matchup Details

See exactly who's at bat and on the mound, including season stats like ERA, Batting Average, and live pitch counts.

03

Real-time Moneyline Odds

Stay on top of the market with live H2H moneyline odds integrated directly into the ticker feed via The Odds API.

04

Official Team Identity

Every team is rendered in its official primary, secondary, and tertiary colors with authentic high-res logos.

05

Productivity First

Docks as a native Windows AppBar. It stays pinned at the top without overlapping your windows, keeping scores in sight while you work.

06

Your Ultimate Setup

Full control over speed, thickness, fonts, and game filters. Show only the teams you care about or follow the whole league.

07

Full Standings Window

Pop open the live standings board to see AL and NL divisions, W-L records, Win %, and Last 10 — all sorted in real-time. Press S to toggle.

08

Total Season Context

Look back at yesterday's finals or peek at tomorrow's probables. MLB-TCKR lets you cycle through dates so you never lose the narrative of the season.

09

Final Game Decisions

Once the game is over, the ticker shows the winning and losing pitchers, their season records, and key final stats for a complete post-game wrap.

10

Watched Team Alerts

Follow specific teams with full-viewport scoring alerts. Get notified the moment they score, when their game starts, and when the final score is in — all rendered in official team colors.
